## Overview

The **Identity Integration Service** consumes `orgmgmt.organization.*` events and executes a provisioning saga against **Zitadel**:
1) Ensure **Organization**,  
2) Ensure **Project**,  
3) Ensure **Project Roles**,  
4) Ensure **OIDC Clients** (Web, Mobile, API/M2M),  
5) Ensure **Service Users**,  
6) Ensure **Policies** (MFA, token, IdP links),  
7) Ensure **Actions** (token claim mapping).

On success, it emits confirmation events (`identity.zitadel.*Ensured`) with external references (IDs) for downstream consumers.
